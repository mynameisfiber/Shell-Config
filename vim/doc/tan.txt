*tan* -- Tangent

  float tan(float arg)~

|tan| returns the tangent of the {arg} parameter. The {arg} parameter is in
radians.

{arg} The argument to process in radians

The tangent of {arg}

|tan| example

<?php >
  echo tan(M_PI_4); // 1
?>
